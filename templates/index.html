SOC Rules of Thumb

  • Verify Domains: Scammers use slight typos (e.g., g00gle.com) or massive subdomains to trick you. Look closely at the URL.
  • Parked Domains: Pages that show "For Sale" or nothing but an error are high risk for temporary scam hosting.
  • Trust The Green: The system looks for SSL validity and active endpoints. Let the engine do the heavy lifting for you.
