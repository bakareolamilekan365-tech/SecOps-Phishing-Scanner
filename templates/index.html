Cyber-Defense Tips

  • 1. Check the domain name carefully. Phishing sites often use slight typos (e.g., g00gle.com instead of google.com).
  • 2. Look for HTTPS. A padlock icon in the address bar is crucial for secure connections, though not foolproof.
  • 3. Beware of odd features. Excessive dots, hyphens, and the '@' symbol in URLs are common phishing tactics.
