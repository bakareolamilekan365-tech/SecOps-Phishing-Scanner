SOC Rules of Thumb
What is a SOC? Security Operations Center: A specialized unit or system (like this AI) dedicated to continuously monitoring, detecting, and neutralizing cybersecurity threats.

Verify Domains
Scammers use slight typos (e.g., g00gle.com) or massive subdomains to trick you. Look closely at the exact URL string.
Spot 'Leetspeak'
Threat actors substitute letters with numbers or symbols (e.g., faceb00k, whats@pp) to bypass brand filters.
Assess Context
If a link arrives unsolicited via SMS, Telegram, or an urgent warning email, treat it as highly suspicious by default.
Is it Parked?
Pages that show "For Sale" or nothing but an error are high risk for temporary scam hosting.
Trust The Engine
